# Research Brief: Menon MediSpa $189 Botox Landing Page

## Target Customer
- **Who**: Women 28-55 in Millburn/Short Hills/Essex County NJ area. Mix of first-timers nervous about trying Botox and experienced patients shopping for a better provider or deal.
- **Trigger**: Seeing wrinkles in photos/Zoom calls, upcoming event, friend's recommendation, or seeing the $189 price in an ad
- **Journey**: First-timers research for 1-7 days. Price-driven shoppers decide quickly (same day). Both want to see credentials and before/afters before booking.

## Top 5 Pain Points (in customer's own words)
1. "I'm afraid I'll look frozen or fake" — fear of unnatural results
2. "How do I know if my injector actually knows what they're doing?" — trust gap
3. "I don't want to be upsold or surprised by the final price" — cost anxiety
4. "I've never done this before and don't know what to expect" — first-timer nerves
5. "There are so many med spas — how do I choose?" — decision paralysis

## Dream Outcome (in their words)
"I want to look like myself, but more refreshed — like I slept really well. I want people to say I look great but not be able to figure out why."

## Top Objections + How We Counter Each
1. **"Will I look frozen?"** → Dr. Menon's artistic approach + before/after gallery showing natural, subtle results
2. **"Is it safe?"** → Board-certified MD, FDA-approved since 2002, physician-administered (not nurse/PA)
3. **"Too expensive"** → $189 for first 20 units vs. $300-400+ at competitors — clear price advantage
4. **"Don't know who to trust"** → Real doctor (not a chain), personally performs every injection, branded before/afters
5. **"What if I don't like it?"** → Wears off in 3-4 months, complimentary consultation first, no obligation

## Competitor Gaps (our opportunity)
- **No one leads with a specific dollar amount** — everyone hides pricing or charges more
- **No one emphasizes doctor-only injections** — most use nurses/PAs
- **No one uses emotional/benefit-driven headlines** — all lead with "Botox in [City]"
- **No one anchors price against market rate** — $189 vs. typical $320+ creates instant value perception
- **No one has the personal touch** — Menon is a real doctor you build a relationship with, not a rotating staff
